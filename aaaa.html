WATER CHARGES

Water is the most basic need of every citizen of the state. Proper utilisation of water and controlling water pollution is main reason for imposing tax on water. Water charges are a widely used economic instrument implied mainly by federal governments to better control water use and water pollution by imposing a price on the use of the environment. The goal of such measures is to internalise negative externalities related to water use. Charges can be placed on emissions, users, the product, or on administrative services. Apart from influencing the amount of consumption, water charges can also activate innovation processes.

ELECTRICITY CHARGES

Electricity is an essential requirement for all facets of our life. It has been recognized as a basic human need. It is a critical infrastructure on which the socio-economic development of the country depends. Supply of electricity at reasonable rate to rural India is essential for its overall development. Equally important is availability of reliable and quality power at competitive rates to Indian industry to make it globally competitive and thus is the important reason for imposing tax. Services sector has made significant contribution to the growth of our economy. Availability of quality supply of electricity is very crucial to sustained growth of this segment

PROPERTY TAX

Property tax is the amount that is paid by the landowner to the municipal corporation or the local government for his/her area. Taxes are a government's principal source of revenue, with the amount of money collected dictating the resources accessible to residents. Every property is a taxable asset, and the property tax is an annual sum paid to the government by the property/landowner.
